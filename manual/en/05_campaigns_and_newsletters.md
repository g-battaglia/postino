# 5. Campaigns & Newsletters

A **Campaign** is a one-time email sent to a specific group of subscribers, like a weekly newsletter or a product announcement.

## Creating a Campaign
1. **Choose an Audience:** You can filter who receives the campaign based on their Tags, Status, or Health Score.
2. **Select a Template:** Choose the visual design for your email.
3. **Write the Content:** Add your subject line and email body.

## Testing and Scheduling
Before you blast an email to thousands of people, Postino requires you to send a **Test Email** to yourself. 

Once you are happy with the result, you can:
- Send the campaign immediately.
- **Schedule** the campaign to be sent automatically at a future date and time.

## Campaign States
A campaign moves through several states: `Draft` ➔ `Scheduled` ➔ `Sending` ➔ `Sent`.
