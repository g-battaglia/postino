# 3. The Web Dashboard

The Web Dashboard is your main control center in Postino. You access it through your web browser by logging in with your administrator account.

## Logging In
Navigate to your Postino URL (e.g., `https://your-domain.com/admin` or similar, depending on setup) and enter your username and password.

## Navigating the Dashboard
Once logged in, you will see a clean, fast interface without heavy loading times. The main sections you can access from the sidebar are:

- **Dashboard / Home:** Shows a high-level overview of your audience, recent growth, and quick stats.
- **Subscribers:** Where you view, search, and edit individual people on your mailing list.
- **Campaigns:** Where you draft and send one-off newsletters.
- **Sequences:** Where you set up automated drip emails (like onboarding).
- **Templates:** Where you visually design the look of your emails.
- **Settings:** Where you configure your email provider and GDPR preferences.

The interface is designed to be intuitive—just click on the section you need and follow the on-screen buttons to create or edit items.
