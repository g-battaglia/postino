# 1. What is Postino?

**Postino** is an open-source, self-hosted email marketing application built primarily for solo developers and small SaaS teams. 

## The Core Philosophy
Unlike many commercial email marketing platforms, Postino is designed from the ground up to respect **privacy** and enforce **GDPR compliance** strictly. 

- **No Sneaky Tracking:** Postino does not use tracking pixels or rewrite your links by default.
- **Append-Only Consent:** When a user agrees to receive your emails, Postino records exactly when and how they consented. This log is never deleted or altered.
- **Your Data, Your Server:** You host Postino on your own infrastructure. You connect it to your preferred email provider (like Resend, Amazon SES, or Mailgun), but you retain complete ownership of your subscriber data.

If you want a simple way to send newsletters, automated onboarding sequences, and track your audience's health—without compromising on privacy—Postino is for you.
