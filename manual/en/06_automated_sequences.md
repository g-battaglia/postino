# 6. Automated Sequences

While Campaigns are one-shot emails, **Sequences** are automated series of emails sent over time. They are often called "drip campaigns."

## Common Use Cases
- **Onboarding:** Send a "Welcome" email immediately when someone signs up, a "Pro Tips" email 2 days later, and a "Feedback Request" email 7 days later.
- **Win-back:** Automatically email users whose "Health Score" drops below a certain threshold.

## How They Work
You define the emails in the sequence and the **delays** between them. Once a subscriber is enrolled in a sequence, Postino handles the rest. As days pass, the system's background tasks will automatically evaluate who is due for their next email and send it out.
