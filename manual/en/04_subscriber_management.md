# 4. Subscriber Management

Your subscribers are the heart of Postino. The Web Dashboard provides powerful tools to manage them effectively.

## Adding Subscribers
From the **Subscribers** page in the dashboard:
- **Manual Entry:** Click "Add Subscriber" to enter an individual's details manually.
- **CSV Import:** Click "Import CSV" to upload a spreadsheet and add large lists at once.

## Tags and Organization
You can assign **Tags** to users (e.g., "pro-plan", "newsletter", "beta-tester"). This allows you to select specific groups when sending campaigns later. You can add tags directly from a subscriber's profile page.

## Health Scores
Postino automatically calculates a **Health Score** (from 0 to 100) for every subscriber. It looks at:
- How long they have been subscribed.
- How often they open or click your emails.
- How long it has been since their last activity.
This helps you easily identify loyal readers and detect those who are losing interest (churning).

## Statuses
Every subscriber has a specific status visible next to their name:
- **Pending:** They signed up but haven't clicked the confirmation link yet (Double Opt-in).
- **Active:** They receive emails normally.
- **Unsubscribed:** They explicitly opted out. (Postino will physically prevent you from sending them more emails).
- **Bounced:** Their email address is invalid or failing.
