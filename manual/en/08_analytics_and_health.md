# 8. Analytics & Health

Sending emails is only half the battle; understanding how they perform is just as important.

## Campaign Analytics
For every campaign you send, Postino tracks:
- **Delivery Rate:** How many emails successfully reached an inbox.
- **Bounces:** How many emails failed to deliver (e.g., the address no longer exists).
- **Spam Complaints:** If someone marks your email as spam, Postino receives an alert from your email provider and automatically suppresses that user to protect your sender reputation.

## Growth and Churn
The dashboard provides a high-level overview of your audience:
- Track how many new subscribers you gain each week.
- Identify **Churn** (subscribers who are unsubscribing or whose Health Scores are plummeting) so you can take action to improve your content.
